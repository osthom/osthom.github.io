DataMule

Data Mule is a website that a fellow Colby student and I made for the Colby Athletics Department. It is a portal for student atheletes, coaches and trainers that allows them to see their weekly workout statistics that are collected from a tool made by Hawkin Dynamics called a force plate. It was built with Flask, MySQL, and Javascript. It was deployed in Fall 2024 and is currently used by the Colby Athletics Department to monitor athlete training. Link To Github
